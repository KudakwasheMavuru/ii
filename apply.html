We tend to be the very first equity investors in startup businesses and often act as lead investors. We provide companies with support before, during, and after the investment. Investment Criteria

Criteria

Make sure you look it up and check if you qualify and start the process as soon as you can.

First funding round

We lead pre-seed rounds & invest in exchange for an % ownership stake.

Clear USP

You must have a strong advantage in an underserved &/or growing market.

Initial traction

You have more than a good concept – the better your chances of securing funding.

Entrepreneurial spirit

You are a capable and ambitious individual who is realistic and flexible about your desired terms.

Any sector

We like diversity and are open to invest in any sector – as long as you meet our other criteria.
